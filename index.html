Family heritage

Three generations.
One name on the side of every bus.

From two buses serving Keispelt and Mamer in 1949, to a family-run group of 525 vehicles and 700 employees today — the Schandeler legacy keeps moving forward.

1949

Founded by Demy

Dominique "Demy" Schandeler takes over the transport business from Camille Misch on January 1st. Two buses, serving Keispelt, Meispelt, Nospelt, Capellen and Mamer.

1965

Demy Schandeler & Fils

Three brothers — Aloyse, Norbert and Josy — take over with mother Catherine after the founder's death. Tourism business begins.

1977

First travel agency

First travel agency opens in Luxembourg City, in collaboration with the Frisch company. Air, rail and cruise travel become available.

2008

Third generation

Joël, Gast and Laurent Schandeler take over management. 170 employees, 120 vehicles, head office in Keispelt and a branch in Eischen.

2019

Cloche d'Or opens

Sixth modern travel agency opens at Cloche d'Or shopping center. New eco-responsible HQ with 3,400 m² of solar panels comes online.

2024

75 years & the Grand Duke

Special anniversary celebration. The Grand Duke himself visits Demy Schandeler, learning about the company's 75-year history and its commitment to the country.
